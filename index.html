junior web developer | london

Hello World! I’ve spent the last 6 years working in the tech industry as a talent scout and began teaching myself how to code.

Software, the principles of UI and UX and what we can build, have always fascinated me. I found myself wanting to grow in an industry that seems to always be evolving, innovating, influencing, and affecting every aspect of society and life as we know it. The more projects I began working on, the more I realised a career in software development is the change and challange I need to feel excited about Mondays again. I decided to take time out in my career and invest in myself and enrolled in General Assembly’s Full-Stack Software Engineering Immersive course to build on what I had self-taught. I hope to join a forward-thinking company as a Junior Developer after my studies.

In lockdown, I've taken an unhealthy interest in American politics and gardening, but before the apocolypse, I quite enjoyed attending tech meetups and exploring far-off lands. In my spare time I'm learning conversational French and volunteer at two NPOs teaching the fundamentals of Software Engineering. It's been an interesting journey so far!

    Bookdoctor is an online GP appointment booking system. The idea is to help ease the phone line occupation and move the booking to an online app. This app has two-way login which allows both GPs and their patients to use the platform. Patients can easily view the available time of their GP and book an appointment. Once booked, they will receive an email confirmation from the app. After the appointment, GP will also be able to leave medical history as a note or prescibe medicine to their patients.
